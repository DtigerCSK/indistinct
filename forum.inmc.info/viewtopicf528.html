Mediator Application Requirements <MUST READ> by DtigerCSK
By submitting an application, you agree that mentioning your application to a staff member will cause it to be immediately denied.

What is a Mediator?

Mediator is our server's version of the well known "Helper" rank. It is a prerequisite to mod that allows us to test your potential and see whether or not you are fit to help run the server. The way it works is simple. If you're a bad mediator, we demote you. If you're a good one, we keep you. If you feel you are worthy enough for mod, you may apply for moderator.

As mediator, you will receive the following permissions:
1. Jail Commands.
2. Kick Commands.
3. Mute Commands.
4. Access to every world.
5. Hawkeye and CoreProtect Commands (No Rollback)
6. Gamemode Commands in every world.

All rules still apply to mediators and higher. Being staff does not exempt you from anything.

Mediator Responsiblities:
1. Answering all questions to the best of your ability.
2. Solving as many problems as you can.
3. Being helpful in general.
4. Jailing and/or muting abusive players.

Requirements:
1. Have at least 100 hours playtime on the server.
2. Been on the server for at least 1 months.
3. Be at least 12 years old.
4. Have never been banned/temp-banned from the server.
(Note: If you have been temp-banned yet you are still helpful, you may deemed worthy enough to still receive the mediator rank.)

Acceptance:
After some time, the staff will discuss the applicants for the mediator rank. If you are deemed worthy of receiving mediator, we will promote you to the mediator rank in-game and let you know that we have accepted you.

Things we look for in an application:
- Good length
- Good Spelling & Grammar
- Demonstration of your maturity

Denials
If you are denied, it is most likely due to one or more of the following reasons in your application and/or in-game:
- Poor spelling/grammar (mostly application only)
- Failure to follow the application format (application only)
- Failure to read and understand all of the information in this post (application only)
- Deficit amount of experience/skill/etc.
- Overall being immature and/or unprofessional
- Excessive use of vulgar and/or offensive speech
- Lack of general server/technological knowledge

You may reapply later on, but if you are being denied too many times and we have warned you not to create more applications, please do not continue to do so.

If your application has not received any response, it's not because we're ignoring you. We watch you in-game and see what you do or don't do, as well as discussing whether or not one is good enough to become a staff member.

While being a mediator, you are expected to live up to the mediator name and do your job correctly. As time goes on while you are a mediator, the staff will take note of the quality of your work. As mediator, you can choose to do the following:

A) Apply for Mod: If you think you are worthy, apply for moderator rank.
B) Stay a Mediator: Stay Mediator for as long as you would like until you apply for mod.
C) Demotion: If deemed unworthy of mediator, we will demote you back to your previous rank.
