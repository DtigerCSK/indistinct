MKX Characters

So you are probably here wondering about the characters that will be next in MKX. Currently we have seven characters confirmed:

+Scorpion
+Cassie Cage
+Kotal Kahn
+Ferra/Torr
+D'Vorah
+Sub-Zero
+Raiden

Raiden was confirmed, but he isn't on the roster, yet. There isn't even a shadowed space for him. This leads me to believe that there will be more than twenty-four characters to choose from on the final roster.

So, I've been doing a lot of wondering... So i created my own character roster for MKX! (Note: SPOILERS! These characters aren't confirmed!)

To start, I have the seven that were confirmed.

Reptile: On the far left it seems that there is a ninja like shadow (compare it to Scorpion on the MKX Roster.) I believe this is Reptile! Reptile never died in MK9. (Could return like Raiden.)

Tanya: Spotted in the background of where you fight Shao Kahn in shackles.

Sonya: Never died in MK9. (Could return like Raiden.)

Johnny Cage: Never died in MK9. (Could return like Raiden.)

Lui Kang (Zombie): Died in MK9, but has resurrected in the past.

Freddy Krueger: Most downloaded character from MK9. Ed Boon made hints about him in MKX. Also there is a shaded out character with a Fedora under Kotal Kahn.

Reiko: Spotted in the background fighting people from MKA on the pit. Could take over for Shao Kahn, since he is the general of Shao Kahn's army, and Shao Kahn died in MK9. (Possible Boss)

Shinnok: Appears at the end of MK9 talking to Quan Chi. Also, Boon said the boss will be a surprise character. This could be Shinnok or Reiko. Maybe even Moloch, Onaga, or Blaze. (I doubt it.)

Quan Chi: Never died. Also if there isn't a new sorcerer in the game, I'm certain Quan Chi will return.

The other characters are shaded out because I couldn't make educated guesses on them. Please comment about your thoughts, and characters you'd like to see or think will be in MKX. (Also the +'s are extra slots.)
