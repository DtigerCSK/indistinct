Plugin Suggestion -- mcMMO

Posted: Thu Jan 09, 2014 9:11 pm
by keagan8808
If you haven't already considered to download and install mcMMO to Indistinct, I would like for you to consider it.

Many servers have used mcMMO for the following:
-Ranks
-Money
-RolePlaying
-Skills
-Much more
I think we could use mcMMO for different abilities to show which skill you are best at. It would definitely be cool to see what other peoples best and most used skills are.

Some typical commands of the plugin are listed below:
/mctop
/mctop [Skill]
/mcstats
/mcrank
/mcability
/mmoedit
/inspect [Player]
-So much more!

Certain commands are used by typical Members, staff, and some may even be disabled for certain players. You can take out abilities if you wish to do so, make sure someone is not cheating somehow, etc.

I hope you download this, for I am certain people will be a bit pleased to see it.

-P.S. I just really like mcMMO
