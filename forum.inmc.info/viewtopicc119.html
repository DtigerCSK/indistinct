Legit Explained

Posted: Sun May 04, 2014 11:21 am
by TheExpertz
Many players of Indistinct are starting to play and enjoy the newly added Legit Survival World world in Indistinct. However, there are lots of questions an rumors about the Legit Survival World. I am here to answer those questions and put those rumors to rest. (To get to the Legit Survival World use /warp legit)

"Where are the Nether and the End?"
I am guessing that the Nether and the End where not added because they were forgotten when the Legit Survival World was released. Do not worry, though. I asked Dtiger about the Nether and the End, and he said that he will be adding them soon.

"Why can I not set more than three homes?"
There is only one rank in the Legit Survival World, and in order for Dtiger to give everyone unlimited homes like we have in the Regular World, he would have to individually give every player unlimited homes, which can become time consuming and frustrating. The only way to simplify this is to move the Legit Survival World to a server.

"How can people use /fly, /god, and /gmc in the Legit Survival World?"
People can use these features by enabling them in the Regular World, then, teleporting to a player in the Legit Survival World. The only way to defeat this is by moving the Legit Survival World to a server.

Well, those are all of the frequently asked questions about the Legit Survival World. Now I have a question for you. Do you want the Legit Survival World moved to another server or not? (Answer the poll above.)

Also, if it were moved to a server, what would you want to see? I would find it great to have my unlimited homes feature back, as well as a currency for the Legit Survival Server where you can buy from a spawn shop, and purchase things such as ranks, saddles, spawn eggs, spawners, etc. If those kind of things were added, they would need to be good price, though. Also, i would find it amazing to have chest shops and warps added to the Legit Survival World.

So what do you want to see? Post your thoughts below!
