Moderator Application Requirements <MUST READ>

Posted: Tue Sep 10, 2013 12:00 am
by DtigerCSK
If you want to become a moderator, before anything else, you need to have been a Mediator for at least 1 Month(Unless we really need moderators).

If you qualify and want to become a moderator on the server, make a new thread with the following in this format:
1. Ingame name:
2. How long have you been playing on the server? (Should be 2 or more months):
3. How long have you been the mediator rank? (Should be at least 1 month):
4. Play time (Should be more than 150 hours):
5. Why you deserve Mod, and what you will do with it. (Make it long):

Good luck, and remember that any un-related posts will be deleted, and if you can't follow this format your application will not be taken seriously.
If you are accepted you may not immediately get Moderator, we will only accept them in times that we need them.
