Admin Application Requirements <MUST READ>

Posted: Tue Sep 10, 2013 12:01 am
by DtigerCSK
Before you can think about leaving an application for Administrator, know that you MUST BE A MODERATOR!

If you feel you are worthy for the highly trusted rank of Admin, then leave an application here on a new thread.

Requirements:
Trust from other members of Staff
Must have been Moderator for at least a month
Ban status not to have changed since your moderator application
Knowledge of Worldedit

You're a Moderator, you should know to follow this format:
1. You ingame name
2. How long you have been moderator (about)
3. Knowledge of WorldEdit
4. Why you want to become a bigger part of staff (Put effort into this)

Good Luck to all applicants and remember, any un-related posts will be deleted, and if you don't follow this
format you will not be taken seriously.

If accepted you may not immediately get your rank!
But you will as soon as we need you.
