Grief Prevention -- New Idea

Posted: Sun Jan 12, 2014 4:04 pm
by keagan8808
Lately, I have discovered a problem. People want to move in towns, but there are a variety of people in different factions, preventing the easiness of just claiming the land so only THEY can use it. There is a plugin, Grief Prevention, that allows you to claim certain land, and trust people, that way, YOU control who has access to the land.

I and Expertz are building a town, GreySnow, and there are already factions that need to be edited. I decided it would be best to make this topic because, well, we most certainly need it (in my opinion).

I would really like this plugin, that way, we can have multiple people from factions, but they can still use the land. It will be less complicated, and who doesn't like less complicated things?

I really hope you take in the consideration, because it will prevent grief of any kind, and allow players to chill with their friends outside of the same faction.

Site -- http://dev.bukkit.org/bukkit-plugins/grief-prevention/
