Building theme: Winter
Anything to do with winter, you can build.

To join:
1) Warp to the contest world with /warp contest
2) Claim a plot with /plotme auto (Must be in contest world!)
3) Build!

Rules:
1) You may build with other players, but only one player will be rewarded if you win.
2) The build needs to be original and new, no copying or rebuilding previous work!

Rewards:
First place - Free rank up along with a steam key for Enclave. If you are a contributor+, choose another player to be ranked up. (Guest-Dedicated -> Contributor, Contributor -> Contributor+) To redeem the steam key you are required to have a steam account. If you do not already have a steam account you can sign up and download it here.
Second place - 100 points. (Don't know what points are? Type /points in game.)
Third place - 75 points + 36 potatoes.

The contest will end on February 1st. Be sure to post a reply here with the following information:
In game name:
Build name: (If any)
Where your plot is: (a warp, coordinates, etc.)
