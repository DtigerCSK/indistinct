But I always come short of those abilities when I'm approached with another front. When the culprit comes on the server, I'm usually left with the victim(s), and myself. And of course, there aren't any staff members around to come by, so I just end up empty handed. Just waiting for staff. I don't want the culprit getting away unnoticed, and I often spot griefs around the server, recent as well. When all you can do is see who did the griefing, there's really not much help done. Other situations where as a player is being rude, all I can do is tell him/her to stop, calm down, etc. With Mediator, I could bring justice to the crime. It's a metaphor.

Another reason is control over abusive/rude players. We all know those types of players who become really big Richards and start going on the offensive with other players. Most off us has been victim and spectators of this problem. At the time, all I can do to those types of players is to tell them to stop. That's it. Stop. Oh and teleport to him/her to see what they're up to. With Mediator, I can either decide to mute or ban the player. Teach the guy a lesson he/she deserves, other than being rendered useless. Sounds a bit cruel, but in my opinion it is the right thing right?

To summarize, to gain Mediator-ship in my opinion would make me more useful to the server and its community. It would impact the server positively, but of course, not too much. I don't like bragging. But whoever supports me in gaining Mediator-ship I would want to thank them! I don't expect to get the rank, but hey, it's worth trying!
