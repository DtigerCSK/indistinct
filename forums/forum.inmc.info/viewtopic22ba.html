Relationships, PDA, and the Server

Posted: Mon Jul 28, 2014 5:28 pm
by Suzu
Alright, I'm probably not the first to SAY it, but I'll be the first to address it: I'm getting really fuckin' SICK of people's IRL love lives' conversations on the server.

Listen, I'm not saying that you shouldn't be in a relationship. Be happy! Life's too damn short to be alone.

But really, don't get so lovey-dovey on the server.

Not only does it depress those players who don't have a significant other, it just gets flat-out annoying. I'll see where players will be confessing their love to one another PUBLICLY in the chat. That's just rude to other players who are also trying to talk to other people about stuff that is more important.

Here's what I'm gonna say: If you are dating someone, on the server or IRL, just /msg that person. If you're too lazy to /msg someone, or you just want people to see that you love them ever so much, then just keep it to yourself. If you can't, then get Skype or Kik or some other kind of social media or messaging app. If you say you don't use social media, then you damn well better get some form of social media.

It would be much appreciated if you took this advice and some initiative to not flood the chat with your public displays of affection. Thank you
