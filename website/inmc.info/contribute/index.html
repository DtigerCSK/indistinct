Contribute

Running servers are not free. Since I cannot cover all the costs myself, we need players on the server to contribute to the fees. As a thank you, we will give you different ranks for different amounts on the main server. Currently, the both servers cost around $39 per month to operate.

Our monthly fees compose of:

  • Minecraft server costs ($35)
  • TeamSpeak 3 server ($2)
  • Website ($1)
  • Domain ($1)

Before you contribute, please read and agree with these terms along with the server rules.

  • There is a no-refund policy, unless you do not receive your perk/perks, then a full refund will be given.
  • You may not misuse your perk, further explained in the rules.
  • The Owners/Admins of the server have the right to remove your perk at any time if you are suspected of misusing it.
  • If you are banned you do not get a refund as you accepted the rules by contributing.
  • If the server suddenly stops and it has been officially announced on THIS website that it has closed down, you will not get a refund.
  • Only if the money was sent by accident, you will get a refund.
  • If you fail to read this and you still donate you will not get a refund for ANY reason

Please note that we do not give out refunds upon your account being banned from our server. You can create a dispute case on our website about getting unbanned. If your unban request is approved, you will retain any perks you had when you were banned. All players are expected and responsible for knowing, understanding, and following all server and website rules.
Even if you are a contributor, you are expected to, and responsible for knowing, understanding, and following, all server and website rules. Failing to oblige to these rules may result in the removal of your rank or permanent ban without refund.
Keep in mind this is a contribution, not a purchase.
